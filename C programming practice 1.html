C programming practice 1

Sharif finished his high school exams, and when the result appeared, he wanted to calculate the number of grades he had lost. Required: Write a program that asks the person about his grades in 6 different subjects, assuming that each subject is out of 100, and then prints a missing score in all subjects

أنهى شريف امتحانات الثانوية العامة، وعندما ظهرت النتيجة أراد أن يحسب عدد الدرجات التي فقدها. المطلوب: كتابة برنامج يسأل الشخص عن درجاته في 6 مواد مختلفة، على افتراض أن كل مادة من 100، ثم يطبع الدرجة المفقودة في جميع المواد

Copied 
    #include<stdio.h>

    int main() {
      int a,b,c,d,e,f;
      
      printf("What score did you get in physics?\n");
      scanf("%d",&a);
      printf("What score did you get in the Arabic language subject?\n");
      scanf("%d",&b);
      printf("What score did you get in the English language subject?\n");
      scanf("%d",&c);
      printf("What score did you get in chemistry?\n");
      scanf("%d",&d);
      printf("What score did you get in mathematics?\n");
      scanf("%d",&e);
      printf("What score did you get in biology?\n");
      scanf("%d",&f);
      int total = a+b+c+d+e+f;
      printf("Total scores : %d\n",total);
      printf("Missing degree = 600 - %d = %d\n",total,600-total);

    }
